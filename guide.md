# MAISON YASMINI — WEBSITE MASTER CONTEXT

## Project Overview

Maison Yasmini is a boutique destination wedding planning brand led by Yasmini, a wedding planner based in Algarve, Portugal.

The brand specializes in intimate and destination weddings for international couples, with a focus on intimate celebrations while remaining open to larger events.

Maison Yasmini is not positioned as a luxury event company for large-scale weddings; instead, it offers highly personalized, elegant and emotional experiences for couples seeking beauty, intimacy and simplicity, led by Yasmini as the principal wedding planner.

The aesthetic is minimal, timeless, sophisticated and chic.

# Founder Story

## About Yasmini

Yasmini lives in Algarve and has over 20 years of experience in the legal field.

After decades dedicated to law, she decided to follow her passion and transform her love for details, organization and meaningful experiences into wedding planning.

Her legal background brings structure, trust and precision to every project, while her passion creates beautiful and emotional celebrations.

Maison Yasmini was born from the belief that love deserves beauty, authenticity and unforgettable moments.
